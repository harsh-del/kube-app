Scaling

Scaling is a feature provided by kubernetes which helps to scale pods through deployment at large scale and even provides us a feature to scale down the pods without disturbing the production enviornment.

Commands used at backened

  • kubectl get deploy
  • kubectl scale deployment {deployment_name} --replicas={no_of_replicas}
